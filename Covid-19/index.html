How To Avoid COVID-19

Some basic Steps We Need To Follow To defeat COVID-19

Step-1

Step-2

Wash Your Hands Atleast 20 seconds Before Leaving & After coming Home From Anywhere..

Step-3

Drink As Much As Water Possible To Be hydrated

Step-4

Please Carry A Hanky And Cover Your Face With It While Sneezing Or If Possible Please Wear A Mask.
